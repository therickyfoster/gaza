Executive Overview

Objective: Mobilize a bilateral, whole-of-nation program where Norway and Taiwan co-develop resilient food systems and secure water cycles—using logistics mastery, public–private sponsorship, and a “hire-the-nation” participation model. Outputs are tracked with open KPIs and neutral, humanitarian guardrails.

Food

Maritime cold-chain reliability, coastal aquaculture upgrades, climate-smart grains/legumes, urban hydroponics/vertical farms, and open seed libraries.

Water

Leak-loss reduction, desal + brine care, aquifer recharge, micro-WASH kits, stormwater reuse, and watershed restoration with digital twins.

People

Every eligible resident can earn via micro-contracts: installs, audits, data-for-good labeling, kitchen gardens, and repair missions.

Program Tracks • What We Build Together

Food Sovereignty Tracks

  • Cold-Chain 2.0: Port-to-plate telemetry, solar-assisted reefers, loss analytics.
  • Blue Foods: Low-impact aquaculture, algae proteins, feed conversion upgrades.
  • Urban Greens: Modular hydroponics; school/community greenhouses; seed libraries.
  • Open Processing: Shared kitchens, mobile mills, rapid safety testing kits.

Water Security Tracks

  • NRW ↓ (Leaks): Citywide leak hunts, pressure zoning, smart meters.
  • Desal + Care: Energy-aware RO with brine blending/valorization pilots.
  • Watershed Repair: Wetland reweaving, upstream sponge measures, soil carbon.
  • Household WASH: Point-of-use filters, safe storage, hygiene kits, micro-maintenance.
Logistics & Sponsorship • The Flywheel

Logistics Bridge

  • Green-fuel maritime lanes for refrigerated goods and spares.
  • Air bridge for perishable vaccines, seeds, sensors, and rapid teams.
  • Digital twin routing with pre-positioned caches and disruption playbooks.

Sponsorship Mix

  • Public mission funds with milestone gates and public dashboards.
  • Corporate co-sponsorship tied to procurement (fridges, filters, meters).
  • Philanthropic challenge grants for open datasets and reference designs.
Hire-the-Nation • How People Earn

Micro-Contracts

  • 5–40 hour tasks (install, audit, survey, teach, test).
  • Stipend + impact bonus; insurance; safety training.
  • Team bounties for greenhouse builds, leak hunts, cold-room retrofits.

Data-for-Good

  • Privacy-preserving labeling: crop stress, algae blooms, pipe hiss detection.
  • Citizen testing logs for water quality; kitchen waste audits for loss reduction.
  • Micro-degrees that unlock higher-tier tasks and fellowships.
